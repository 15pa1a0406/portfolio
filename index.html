CRICKET

TOP PLAYERS

Sachin

Sachin Ramesh Tendulkarborn 24 April 1973) is a former Indian international cricketer and a former captain of the Indian national team, regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time. He is the highest run scorer of all time in International cricket. Often referred to as the 'God of Cricket' by the Indian cricket fans.

Rahul

Rahul Dravid (born 11 January 1973) is a former Indian cricketer and captain, widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He is the current Overseas Batting Consultant for the Indian team, and also the head coach for the Under-19 and 'A' teams.

Ganguly

Sourav Chandidas Ganguly born 8 July 1972, affectionately known as Dada (meaning "elder brother" in Bengali), is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team, Currently, he is appointed as the President of the Cricket Association of Bengal and he is from Bengal.

Lakshman

Vangipurapu Venkata Sai Laxman born 1 November 1974, commonly known as V.V.S. Laxman (often VVS, and sometimes as "Very Very Special"), is a former Indian cricketer and currently a cricket commentator.Laxman was an aggressive right-hand batsman who played mostly in the lower middle order.He is from Andhra Pradesh.
